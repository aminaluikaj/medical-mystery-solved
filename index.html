Welcome to Medical Mystery Solved

A club and organization dedicated to researching medical anomalies, raising awareness about rare diseases, and promoting health education.

About Us

Medical Mystery Solved is a New Dorp High School club with an international organization component. We collaborate on medical research, host discussions, and work on projects that explore fascinating medical mysteries.

Our Mission

  • Investigate medical cases and rare diseases
  • Raise awareness about chronic illnesses
  • Engage in research projects and discussions
  • Provide educational resources for students and the community
